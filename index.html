Sambar Podi

Make Delicious Sambar with Kai Manam Sambar Podi!

Aromatic and perfectly balanced masala made from premium whole spices — freshly roasted and ground in our kitchen. Suitable for all age groups with a balanced spice level.

🌶️ Ingredients

Coriander seeds, dried red chillies, toor dal, chana dal, black pepper, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, turmeric, curry leaves

🍲 How to Use

Add 1–2 tablespoons while cooking sambar. Mix well and allow to boil. Can also be used in curries, kootu and gravies.

📦 Storage Tips

Store in a cool dry place. Keep tightly closed after use. Refrigerate after opening. Always use a dry spoon.

⭐ What Makes It Special

Freshly roasted and ground in small batches. Balanced spice level. Traditional recipe. Made with whole spices only.

Idli Podi

A Perfect Blend of Tradition & Authentic Taste!

A classic South Indian staple — perfect with gingelly oil or ghee. Light on the stomach, rich in taste. Made with whole spices using the same method used in traditional Tamil homes.

Choco Nutri Mix

A Healthier Way to Happiness!

A delicious blend of millets, nuts, cocoa and natural sweetness. Rich in calcium and protein. Ideal for children, students and anyone who wants a wholesome and tasty drink.

🍫 Ingredients

Ragi, wheat, roasted gram, almonds, cashews, pistachios, cocoa powder, cardamom, jaggery powder

⭐ What Makes It Special

Made with millets and real dry fruits. Natural jaggery sweetener. No refined sugar. Loved by children and adults alike.

Moringa Soup Powder

A Natural Way to Good Health!

A nutritious herbal blend with moringa leaves as the hero ingredient. Iron-rich, light on the stomach and easy to prepare. A warm bowl every morning can make a real difference to your health.

🌿 Ingredients

Moringa leaves powder, pepper, cumin seeds, garlic, ginger, turmeric, coriander, salt

🍲 How to Use

Add 1–2 teaspoons to 1 cup of water. Boil for 3–5 minutes. Stir well. Adjust salt to taste. Serve hot.

📦 Storage Tips

Store in airtight container away from sunlight. Cool and dry place. Use dry spoon. Best within 2 months of opening.

⭐ What Makes It Special

Rich in iron and calcium from pure moringa leaves. Light and easy to digest. Supports daily immunity naturally.
