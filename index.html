Sambar Podi

Make Delicious Sambar with Kai Manam Sambar Podi!

Aromatic and perfectly balanced sambar masala made from premium whole spices — freshly roasted and ground for rich aroma. Suitable for all age groups with a balanced spice level.

🌶️ Ingredients

Coriander seeds, dried red chillies, toor dal, chana dal, black pepper, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, turmeric, curry leaves

🍲 How to Use

Add 1–2 tablespoons while cooking sambar. Mix well and allow to boil for rich flavor. Can also be used in curries, kootu and gravies.

📦 Storage Tips

Store in a cool dry place. Keep tightly closed. Refrigerate after opening for longer freshness. Always use a dry spoon.

⭐ Why Special

Freshly roasted and ground. No preservatives. No artificial colors. Authentic homemade taste with traditional recipe.

Idli Podi

A Perfect Blend of Tradition & Authentic Taste!

A classic South Indian staple — perfect to mix with gingelly oil or ghee. Rich in taste, light on the stomach. Made with 100% natural ingredients using traditional roasting method.

Roasted gram dal, urad dal, dried red chillies, sesame seeds, garlic, curry leaves, asafoetida, salt

Moringa Soup Powder

A Natural Way to Good Health!

A nutritious blend of moringa leaves, spices and natural ingredients. Rich in iron and nutrients. Supports immunity and overall health. Light and easy to digest for all age groups.

🌿 Ingredients

Moringa leaves powder, pepper, cumin seeds, garlic, ginger, turmeric, coriander, salt

🍲 How to Use

Add 1–2 teaspoons to 1 cup of water. Boil for 3–5 minutes and stir well. Adjust salt if needed. Serve hot as healthy soup.

📦 Storage Tips

Store in airtight container away from sunlight. Keep in cool dry place. Use dry spoon. Best consumed within 2 months of opening.

⭐ Why Special

Rich in iron and calcium from moringa. Supports immunity. Light and easy to digest. No preservatives. Traditional preparation.
